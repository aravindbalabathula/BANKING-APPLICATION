FOUNDERS

PROFFESSOR

Professor, portrayed by Álvaro Morte, is the brilliant and meticulous mastermind behind the heists in "Money Heist." Often staying one step ahead of the authorities. His calm demeanor, unwavering focus, and ability to adapt under pressure make him a central and captivating character in the series.

TOKYO

Tokyo, played by Úrsula Corberó, is a complex character known for her fiery spirit and emotional depth. She serves as the narrator of the series and is a key member of the heist crew. Tokyo's determination and leadership qualities, along with her complicated past, add depth to her character, making her a fan favorite.

BERLIN

Berlin, portrayed by Pedro Alonso, is the enigmatic and charismatic leader of the group of robbers. His charm, sophistication, and ruthlessness make him a captivating but morally ambiguous character. Berlin's complex personality and relationships with other characters contribute to the intrigue and tension of the series.
