  • Loan Amount: The borrower agrees to the loan amount specified in the loan agreement.
  • Interest Rate: The borrower acknowledges the fixed or variable interest rate associated with the loan.
  • Repayment Schedule: The borrower agrees to the repayment schedule, including the frequency (e.g., monthly, quarterly), starting date, and the number of installments.

  • Use of Funds: The borrower acknowledges that the loan funds will be used for the purpose specified in the agreement (e.g., education, home purchase, business expansion).
